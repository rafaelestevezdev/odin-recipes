Margherita Pizza

Description

The Margherita Pizza is the quintessential Neapolitan pizza, featuring a thin crust topped with fresh mozzarella cheese, vibrant tomato sauce, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil. It's simple but delicious, allowing the quality of the ingredients to shine.

Ingredients

  • 250g (9oz) pizza dough
  • 200g (7oz) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 150g (5oz) tomato sauce (preferably crushed tomatoes or passata)
  • 1 cup (100g) Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (475°F) or as hot as it can go.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface until you achieve your desired size and thickness.
  3. Place the dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Arrange the slices of mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  6. Season with a pinch of salt and drizzle a little olive oil on top.
  7. Bake the pizza for 8-10 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Once out of the oven, add fresh basil leaves and a final drizzle of olive oil before serving.
